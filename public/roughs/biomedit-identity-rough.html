Brand Identity

A first look at type and color for BiomEdit.

Below are three typeface options — DM Sans, PT Sans, and Montserrat — each shown as a complete set: sizes, headlines paired with body copy, pull quotes, paragraphs, captions, and small labels, so you can see how each one behaves across everything the brand needs to do, not just in a headline. A serif alternative (Fraunces) is layered in as a possible accent for quotes and captions only. After type, you'll find the brand colors, a proposed complementary accent, neutral gray options, and how the colors apply to buttons and links.

Confirmed direction — keeping Ember Orange as the warm counterpoint to the two blues.
